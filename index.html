Croissants

FUN FACT! Made from layer upon layer of flakey pastry, loads of butter, and tasting like heaven, the croissant (pronounced “kwa-son”) is simply a thing of joy and wonder. Since the 19th-century, croissants have been an important part of breakfast in various countries and with different variations in fillings and toppings.
